HR Analytics Data Analysis Project

Overview

HR attrition analysis is the process of studying employee data to understand how many people are leaving a company and why they are leaving. The goal is to find patterns and reasons behind employee exits and to help the company improve employee retention.

This analysis includes:

  • Calculating the attrition rate
  • Checking if employees left voluntarily or were let go (involuntarily)
  • Studying attrition by department, age, salary, and other factors

Business Problem

This HR analytics project aims to understand the key factors influencing employee attrition. The analysis focuses on:

  • Work experience
  • Promotion duration
  • Income levels
  • Departments
  • Job roles
  • Job satisfaction

By examining these factors, the project seeks to identify patterns and trends related to why employees leave the company. The insights gained can help HR teams improve employee retention, enhance job satisfaction, and make better data-driven decisions.
